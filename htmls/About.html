About us

Virtual Socialites is a non-profit website intended for students to socialize and have fun with each other outside of class hours. It will keep track of any upcoming events as well as have chat lobbies per event where they can chat with fellow students. In the age of social distancing, quarantine, and online classes, it's important to make sure that we are still socializing with each other in some manner and Virtual Socialites will help with that.
